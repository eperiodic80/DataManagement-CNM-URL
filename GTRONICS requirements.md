## 5.6. Summary of the management plan of the planned data
The specific data plan will be generated at the start of the project according to FAIR criteria (Findable,
Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable) and according to the guidelines of the institutions (UVA and
CSIC), this call, and FPI fellowship program (if the project was granted with some PhD student) and/or
the university where the FPI fellow will be enrolled. The data management plan will be linked to the
Plan for Scientific Communication, the Plan for Dissemination of the Results and the Plan for the
Transfer and Valorization of Results.
GTRONICS will generate (a) protocols on technical methods, (b) experimental data and observational
data, (c) device layouts and their fabrication flow, (d) equipment-specific data (e) multimedia
documents, and (f) images and videos. No data on humans is expected.
The manufacturing of samples in the SBCNM is traced through internal applications (BACO) that track
the processes of the samples throughout their fabrication. Specific templates will also be put in place
to group all the generated data and documentation. This data will be owned by the institution
generating it. The project will use the cloud platform by CSIC (SACO) to save and share data efficiently
and to promote discussion during the life of the project, and the institutional repository (DIGITAL.CSIC)
to organize, preserve and to provide open access to the research outputs.
# 2. JUSTIFICATION AND CONTRIBUTION OF COORDINATION
The management work package in the work plan includes different activities devoted to coordination.
The interaction between the teams will be constant and fluent, with short periodic weekly meetings
and semi-annual consortium meetings. Periodic revisions of the plans and the progress of the project
will be jointly carried out A common data management plan will be generated, which will also include
the protocols for the monitoring and interchange of samples between partners.
